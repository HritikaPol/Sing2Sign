About Us

Empowering Communication with Momo

Our Vision

To create a world where communication is inclusive and seamless for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by leveraging technology and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between spoken and sign language using AI-driven solutions that convert voice inputs into expressive sign language through animated avatars, making communication more accessible and natural.

Motivation Behind the Project

Communication is a basic human right, yet millions in the deaf community face barriers every day. Our inspiration came from observing how difficult it is for hearing-impaired individuals to interact in a world designed for spoken language. We wanted to build a bridge — something meaningful, impactful, and inclusive. "Momo" is more than just a project; it's our step toward equal communication for all.
